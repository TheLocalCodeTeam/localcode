How agents work

1

Describe your task

Just tell Localcode what you need. No agent selection required.

2

Auto-dispatch

Localcode analyzes your task and automatically activates the right agents based on keywords and context.

3

Orchestrate

Multiple agents work in parallel through the NEXUS pipeline with quality gates between each phase.

4

Synthesize

All agent outputs are synthesized into a coherent result. Failed tasks are automatically retried.
